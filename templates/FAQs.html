FAQs

Q) How much time does RailYatri take to initiate refund?

A: On confirmation of the cancellation, RailYatri initiates the refund within 24 Hours.



Q) How much time does it take for the money to reflect in my bank account?

A: Once the refund is initiated from our end, it takes 2-3 working days in case of Private Bank or SBI and 5-7 working days in case of other Banks. However, refunds in our e-wallet - RY Wallet is instant.



Q) What should I do if my money is not reflected in the given time lines?

A: Use the refund ID given by us to speak to your Bank/Payment Gateway and know the status of your refund easily.
